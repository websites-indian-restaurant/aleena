About Aleena

ALEENA Indian Restaurant is Indian, Asian, Balti, Eastern European cuisine dedicated to providing the customer with an exceptional overall dining experience to moderate prices, consistently.

This restaurant is full of Light, colors and that festive ambience which makes our customers relax and enjoy their meal. With a buzzy atmosphere and a healthy competition in the hospitality industry it is time to try something difference from the Chicken Tikka Masala and Kathmandu Kitchen is here to provide its customers that difference. We believe only in excelling and so do our friendly staffs, unique Nepalese hospitality that will make you return soon for another tantalizing “taste”.
