About Aleena

About Aleena

Established in 1993 by Mr. Azad Khan, Aleena Restaurant was given the name of AFSANA Tandoori Restaurant, due to its unique home made recipe's, family atmosphere and great tourist location, AFSANA took over the charge of whole indian restaurant market in the area.

In 2013, on completion of its 20 years service, ALEENA INDIAN RESTAURANT replaces Afsana Tandoori with its new management. In the heart of Dublin city with its expensive competitors, ALEENA launches its very affordable 2-course, 4-course and 5-course special menus.

To meet the best quality of food and service Aleena choose unique and the best Tandoori, Balti, Baryani and traditional curry dishes of India and the sub continent. To visit Aleena Restaurant is always the best choice for anyone who likes to have a selection of flavoured indian food mild to very hot.
